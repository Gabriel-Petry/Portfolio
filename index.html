About Me

      Hi! I’m João Gabriel Petry de Brito, a tech nerd and a developer of random things from Sapucaia do Sul, Brazil. I'm passionate about turning ideas into codes, I REALLY love all the logic behind everything. With a solid foundation in technologies like Java, HTML, PHP, JavaScript, CSS, and MySQL, I enjoy working across all the languages and diving deep into how systems work behind the scenes, I'm still looking for a specific language to specialize myself. I'm currently studying Systems Analysis and Development at Unisinos, a journey that’s been both exciting and transformative. All the semesters come with a new challenge that helps me to improve not just as a developer, but as a problem-solver and lifelong learner. I'm also investing in my communication skills by studying English, Russian and soon to begin German aiming to collaborate with international teams and explore opportunities abroad. What drives me is curiosity, creativity, and a desire to build cool and impressive things that matter. Whether it's a big or small project, I’m always ready to take on the next project with energy and focus. I'm ready to learn and adapt to new environments!

Let's build something great together!
